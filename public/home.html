What is Weightlifter?

Weightlifter is a website made to make programming as simple as possible. With so many different programs floating around online, our goal is gather them all and put them in one place. We'll take your numbers and tell you all the weights for the program, so you don't have to do any of the work. Browse programs for free, or sign up and save a program to track your progress.
